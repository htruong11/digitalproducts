# Executor First 30 Days Estate Admin Kit

Higher-ticket product idea from IMP-006.

Status: parked until legal-adjacent claims are de-risked.

Allowed future framing:

- Administrative tracker only: contacts, death certificate count, document
  inventory, bills/accounts log, calls made, attorney notes, and family updates.

Blocked framing:

- Probate instructions, tax guidance, asset-transfer advice, legal
  interpretations, deadlines, or any promise to settle an estate.
