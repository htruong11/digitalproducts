# Care Pact Sibling Coordination Kit

Next library PDF from IMP-007.

Positioning:

- Buyer: adult siblings coordinating aging-parent care across uneven schedules,
  errands, calls, and updates.
- Promise: family coordination and task ownership only.
- Status: build-ready draft; publish only after the standard claims scrub and
  listing image review.

Allowed framing:

- Organizer, worksheet, planner, task board, family check-in, update template,
  and care coordination.

Blocked framing:

- Legal agreement, enforceable contract, family mediation advice, medical
  advice, reimbursement advice, tax guidance, or any promise to reduce conflict
  or improve care outcomes.
