# Caregiver Product Research Resource (2026)

This document is a living resource for agents to inform product development, marketing copy, and design decisions for the **Family Caregiver Command & Facility System (FCCS)**.

## 1. Market Trends & High-Demand Features (Etsy 2026)

### A. Outcome-Driven Ecosystems
*   **The "Bundle" is King:** Move beyond single trackers. Sell "Ecosystems" that combine administration (trackers) with emotion (journals) and automation (AI prompts).
*   **Digital Dashboards:** Massive shift toward **Notion** and **Google Sheets** for real-time family updates.
*   **Condition-Specific Kits:** High demand for ADHD/Neurodivergent adult care, Post-Op recovery bundles, and Dementia "reminiscence" packs.

### B. "Rewired Wellness" (The Emotional Wedge)
*   **Dopamine Menus:** 5/15/30-minute self-care resets for caregivers in high-stress environments.
*   **Anxiety Reset Cards:** Grounding techniques for quick access on mobile during crises.
*   **Shadow Work Journals:** Focused on the "Invisible Load" and grief.

### C. 2026 Aesthetic: "Home as a Sanctuary"
*   **Palette:** Warm, grounded tones (Burnt Mahogany, Plum Berry, Grounded Greens). Avoid clinical blue/grey.
*   **Style:** Hand-drawn elements and neomorphism (tactile, soft shadows) to counter "AI slop."
*   **Accessibility:** 12pt+ fonts, high contrast, mobile-native ratios.

## 2. Evidence-Based Logic (The "Authority" Pillar)

### A. The Clinical EHR Gap
*   **Finding:** EHRs miss **90% of geriatric syndromes** (falls, delirium, malnutrition).
*   **Product Opportunity:** The "Doctor Snapshot" should focus on Patient-Generated Health Data (PGHD) that EHRs ignore.
*   **Frameworks:** Use the **4Ms** (Medications, Mentation, Mobility, What Matters) and **5Ms** (+ Multimorbidity).

### B. Sibling Equity Dynamics
*   **The Equity Paradox:** Families striving for 50/50 splits (Equality) burn out faster than those using **Equity** (fairness based on expertise/ability).
*   **The Proximity Penalty:** Local siblings bear the brunt; non-local siblings feel "information asymmetry" or guilt.
*   **Tooling:** Sibling Equity Trackers should visualize contributions to move families from childhood roles to adult expertise.

### C. The Economic Valuation
*   **Finding:** Informal caregiving is worth **2.4% of global GDP**.
*   **Marketing Angle:** Position the kit as a tool to reclaim "Invisible Labor" and professionalize family care.

## 3. B2B & Facility Compliance (FCCS Pro)

### A. Regulatory Milestones (2026)
*   **FGI 2026 Mandate:** **90% single-occupancy rooms** for infection control and privacy in new residential care settings.
*   **Digital Accessibility:** **WCAG 2.1 Level AA** compliance is mandatory by **April 24, 2026**, for public-facing digital tools.
*   **Building Codes:** IBC/IRC 2026 Group R-4 classification for 3–16 residents.

### B. Operational Safety
*   **"Survey-Ready" Logs:** Need for timestamped compliance logs (water temp, fire safety, staff handoffs).
*   **The "One-Minute Handoff":** Structured protocol to reduce cognitive load during shift changes.
