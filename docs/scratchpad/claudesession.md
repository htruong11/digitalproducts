The Core Issue
Canva has two types of elements inside its design tool — free elements and Pro elements (the ones with a crown symbol). The licensing rules differ significantly when you're selling products commercially.
What You CAN Do

Use Canva to design templates and sell them on Etsy
Use free Canva elements in products you sell
Sell Canva templates where the buyer edits the template themselves (you share a Canva link, they customize it)
Use Pro elements in designs you sell as long as the buyer is also a Canva Pro user editing it themselves

What You CANNOT Do

Use Canva Pro elements in a finished PDF you sell for download — that violates the license because you're distributing the element itself, not just the template
Claim copyright ownership of Canva's stock elements
Resell or redistribute Canva's photos, graphics, or fonts as standalone assets
