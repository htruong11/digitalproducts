# IMP-002: The Family Caregiver Command & Facility System (FCCS)

## Abstract

Elevate the digital product line from a static "PDF kit" to a high-value **Transformation System** serving both family caregivers (B2C) and boutique micro-residential facilities (B2B). 

The **FCCS** is a scientifically-grounded coordination infrastructure designed to solve the "Invisible Load" of caregiving and the "Regulatory Anxiety" of facility ownership. By anchoring every feature in scholarly evidence—such as the 90% clinical gap in elder care records and the economic valuation of informal labor—we position this product suite as the authoritative leader in the 2026 caregiving market.

## 1. The Evidence Base (Authority & Research)

To ensure market dominance, the FCCS is built upon four pillars of 2024–2026 research:

### A. The Clinical Gap (The "Why")
*   **Finding:** Standard EHRs miss up to **90% of geriatric syndromes** (falls, malnutrition, isolation) (Source: NIH, 2024).
*   **Application:** Our "Doctor Snapshot" uses structured PGHD (Patient-Generated Health Data) to reduce clinician interpretation time by **25%** and surface these hidden risks (Source: JMIR, 2025).

### B. The Sibling Equity Paradox (The "Resilience")
*   **Finding:** Families striving for strict **Equality** (50/50 splits) suffer higher burnout. Success is found in **Equity** (fairness based on resource/ability) (Source: Within-Family Differences Study, 2024).
*   **Application:** The "Sibling Equity Tracker" quantifies and visualizes contributions to move families from childhood roles to adult expertise-based coordination.

### C. The Economic Valuation (The "Value")
*   **Finding:** Informal caregiving is worth **2.4% of global GDP** but remains "invisible" (Source: Cambridge/Eurocarers, 2024).
*   **Application:** Our "Care Penalty Calculator" uses the Replacement Cost Method to help caregivers negotiate for financial or operational support.

### D. The Mastery Buffer (The "Prevention")
*   **Finding:** **Self-Mastery** is the strongest psychological buffer against burnout. Mindfulness-Based Interventions (MBIs) are the gold standard for burden reduction (SMD -6.67) (Source: NIH, 2025).
*   **Application:** Micro-courses focus on "Medical Task Mastery" to increase caregiver confidence and prevent psychological fatigue.

## 2. Lean Content Design & Checklist Principles

To ensure FCCS products are "succinct and helpful" under stress, we apply the following 2026 Health Literacy and Cognitive Load (CLT) standards:

*   **The Three A's Framework (CDC 2026):**
    *   **Accurate:** Evidence-based without jargon (e.g., "confusion" instead of "mentation change").
    *   **Accessible:** 12pt+ font, high contrast, mobile-native aspect ratios.
    *   **Actionable:** Every checklist item must start with a verb (e.g., "Check" or "Call") and focus on the immediate next step.
*   **Extraneous Load Reduction:** Limit checklists to **3–7 items** per category. Use "White Space" as a functional element to prevent visual crowding.
*   **The "Cognitive Anchor" Principle:** For ER/Hospital situations, focus on orientation items (e.g., analog clocks, hearing aids) that prevent delirium—a major cause of ER misdiagnosis (90% gap).
*   **The "One-Minute Handoff" (CLT Scaffolding):** A structured protocol for shift changes that uses "GenAI Scaffolding" to summarize daily logs into three bullet points: (1) Vital Changes, (2) Meds Given, (3) Emotional State.
