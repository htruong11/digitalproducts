# IMP-004: The Boutique Care Facility Starter Kit (B2B Extension)

## Abstract

Expand the FCCS ecosystem into the B2B market by targeting the **Micro-Residential / Boutique Care Home** sector. This document leverages the upcoming care home project of your friend as a "Design Partner" and "Validation Site."

In 2026, small facilities (6-12 beds) face a "Staffing Crisis" and "Regulatory Complexity" that makes high-quality documentation a life-or-death requirement for the business. The decision is to create a **"Facility Starter Kit"**—a professionalized version of our caregiver system designed for small-scale care home owners.

## 1. The B2B Opportunity (May 2026 Context)

- **The "Design Partner" Advantage:** Your friend's care home serves as the "Alpha Test" site. We can observe real-world friction in staff handoffs, medication audits, and resident family communication.
- **Target Audience:** Owners of Residential Assisted Living (RAL) or RCFE (Residential Care Facilities for the Elderly) with 1-3 homes.
- **Revenue Math:** While the consumer PDF kit sells for $14-$47, a B2B "Facility Starter Kit" can be priced at **$497-$1,497** as a "Business-in-a-Box."

## 2. Product Components: The Facility Extension

### A. The "Survey-Ready" Documentation Hub (Google Sheets/Notion)

Small homes suffer from "Survey Anxiety" (state inspections). Our system provides:

- **Daily Safety Log:** Automated timestamped checks for water temperature (<120°F), fire exits, and HVAC.
- **Medication Audit Sheet:** A logic-gated sheet that flags "missing signatures" or "late doses" for the administrator before they become citations.
- **The "Person-First" Care Plan:** A digital template that converts resident goals into the "4Ms" format required by 2026 Age-Friendly standards.

### B. The "Invisible Load" Staffing Toolkit

To solve the burnout of small care teams:

- **Voice-to-Text Handoff Generator:** A mobile-friendly form where caregivers dictate their shift summary, and AI parses it into structured clinical notes.
- **Equity Dashboard (Professional Version):** Tracks staff shift balance and highlights high-performers to reduce turnover.

### C. The Family Transparency Portal

A major differentiator for a boutique home is **communication**:

- **Weekly AI Summary:** A tool that takes daily logs and generates a "Family Newsletter" style update for the resident's children, summarizing health, social activity, and "What Matters" goals.
- **The "4Ms" Scorecard:** A monthly visual report given to families to show the care home's impact on their parent's well-being.

## 3. Synergy with Your Friend's Care Home

- **Validation:** Use the friend's facility to capture "raw" staff feedback on usability.
- **Social Proof:** "Tested and implemented at [Friend's Care Home Name], a premier boutique facility."
- **Referral Engine:** Every family that has a resident in the care home becomes a potential advocate for our consumer products (B2C) for their friends who aren't in a facility yet.

## 4. 2026 Trend Integration: "Invisible Infrastructure"

- **Camera-Free Monitoring Integration:** The Facility Kit will include a "Buyer's Guide" for radar-based fall detection (like Helpany), positioning our documentation as the "Control Center" for these passive sensors.
- **HIPAA-Compliant "Airtable/Notion" Setup:** Step-by-step instructions for a small business to set up a secure, compliant cloud environment without a $50k enterprise EHR.
